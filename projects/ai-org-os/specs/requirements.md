# Requirements (MVP)

> 想定読者: ai-org-os の仕様策定・実装・レビュー担当。
> このドキュメントは [Product Vision](../../../docs/product_vision.md) の 3 軸（Build / Maintain / Share）に整合する。

1. The repository must be understandable by both humans and AI agents.
2. The organization structure must be explicit in Markdown.
3. Team roles must have clear responsibilities.
4. Decisions must be recorded.
5. Retrospectives must produce learnings or rule changes.
6. The product must be able to use itself as its first development process (dogfooding as a verification means, not as the goal).
7. No runtime application is required in the first commit.
8. The product must be able to emit the constructed AI team as a portable **L1 bundle** (Share axis).
9. A self-improvement loop (decisions → retrospectives → learnings → rule updates) must be defined to ensure maintainability (Maintain axis).
